Investigation Overview¶

The exploration focused on the reason behind taking a loan and what are Prosper ratings and what the customers of Prospers do for a living

Dataset Overview¶

The data consists 113,937 loans from prosper with 81 variables on each loan. The subset of the dataset contains 113,066 loans and 13 variable that we will focus on in our analysis.

The Prosper ratings¶

Prosper use a special metric they made to measure the ability of customers paying their loans without defualt, The ratings goes as follow : AA,A,B,C,D,E,HR , where AA is the safest choice for Prosper and HR is high risk customer in terms of completing the loan payments without a default.

What is the most common reason to take a loan ?¶

The number one reason that make a person take a loan, despte wether he is from the AA rated group or HR group is debt consolidation, which means that instead of having diffrent debts with diffrent intrest rates, you can group them into one larger loan istead of having multiple loans and debts.
